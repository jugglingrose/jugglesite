XYZ Juggling

Annual Juggle Festival

Each year we hold our annual juggling festival. This annual event brings in jugglers from around the states and from overseas. It's a three day event jam packed with juggling fun ranging from games, competitions, workshops, and shows.

Held at XYZ Auditorium @ 555 somewhere lane. Passes are sold at the event. Weekend pass is $40 or purchase a one day pass for $25. Evening shows are free and open to the public.
